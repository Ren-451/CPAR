ARTIST

Biggest Challenge: During the pandemic, she was excited with all her time. Lanot had opened a shop, held online workshops, did personal work, and more. After three years of non-stop work, she dealt with burnout, so 2023 was a hard year for her. She had done so much, but her body could not carry on. So, she stopped and rested. She describes how our bodies are similar to nature. There are different seasons, and we have to adjust to them.

May 19, 2018

How to deal with creative block: Lanot defined creative block as your body telling you to stop. She suggests going out, doing manicures, journaling, and more.

February 19, 2024

Hundertwasser is one of Lanot’s art heroes. Though their art styles aren't the same, she admires the artist’s courage to share their ideas unapologetically, no matter how crazy or weird their work is. Another art hero she mentioned was Henri Matisse.

September 29, 2019

Color palettes: She is inspired by nature. The colors are bright and joyful. She likes happy colors.
